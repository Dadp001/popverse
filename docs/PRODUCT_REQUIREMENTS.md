# PopVerse Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Elevator Pitch

PopVerse is a unified social platform for discovering, tracking, rating, reviewing, discussing, and celebrating every form of entertainment.

Instead of maintaining separate accounts across multiple services for movies, books, games, comics, music, theatre, live events, and discussions, users can manage their entire entertainment life in one place.

## Problem Statement

Entertainment is fragmented.

Users today need multiple applications to:

- Track movies
- Track TV shows
- Track books
- Track manga
- Track comics
- Track games
- Track music
- Discuss entertainment
- Discover upcoming releases
- Build recommendations
- Share lists

There is currently no platform that unifies every entertainment medium into one ecosystem while combining discovery, community, ratings, discussions, personalized recommendations, and yearly community awards.

PopVerse aims to solve this problem.
